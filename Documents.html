Project Charter

30th January 2023

In the context of our project, 'The Future of Crime Prevention: Police Case Analysis Using Machine Learning,' a project charter serves as a concise, comprehensive document outlining the project's core elements. It encompasses the project's objectives, Sub objectives, execution strategy, and stakeholder involvement. The project charter plays a pivotal role in project planning, providing guidance throughout the project's entire lifecycle. This charter, typically a concise document, serves as a reference point for more extensive documents like new offering requests or proposals. Its primary purpose is to meticulously document key aspects, including the rationale behind initiating the project, the project's goals and limitations, strategies for achieving the solution, and anticipated project benefits. Additionally, it addresses the commercialization aspects of the project, particularly concerning its service offerings.

Proposal Report

Draft : 24th March 2023

Final : 05th May 2023

In the realm of project management, a project proposal stands as a foundational document that plays a pivotal role in securing support from sponsors and stakeholders. This essential document outlines the problem that requires attention and elaborates on the benefits that solving it can bring. Essentially, a project proposal serves as the initial blueprint for giving life to the project's concept, providing insights into the objectives and the strategies for their attainment. It's a common practice for a project proposal to encompass a comprehensive list of activities or tasks linked to the project, demonstrating the significance of the project idea in question while shedding light on its origins. The creation of a proposal serves the critical purpose of enabling an organization to deliver a structured, logical presentation to an external worker or project benefactor. Typically, proposals take shape in the early project phases, offering a solid foundation for discussion and planning before detailed project plans are finalized and resources are allocated.

Status Documents

Status Document 1: 22nd May 2023

Status Document 2: 6th September 2023

Research Status Documents serve as routine updates disseminated to both management and project teams, providing comprehensive insights into project statuses, potential challenges, and milestones reached. By consistently keeping all group members informed through weekly, monthly, or quarterly updates, these documents foster accountability and ensure alignment of resources when necessary. Throughout the course of our project, two critical status documents were submitted. The first status document submitted during Progress Presentation 1, and the second status document submitted during Progress Presentation 2. Status Document 1 encapsulates all relevant meeting details up to the Progress Presentation 1 deadline, while Status Document 2 encompasses meeting-related information up to the Progress Presentation 2 deadline. These documents serve as repositories for recording a myriad of interactions, including group meetings, supervisor consultations, discussions, MS Teams meetings, WhatsApp exchanges, and email correspondences. This holistic approach ensures that all aspects of our project's communication and decision-making processes are meticulously documented.

Final Reports

10th September 2023

A Project Report is a comprehensive document that offers a comprehensive overview of a proposed project. This report delves into the projects overall plan and execution, evaluating the viability of the intended plan or activity. It encapsulates essential data that underpins the project's appraisal and feasibility assessment. The report covers various aspects, including the project's introduction, requirements, methodology, algorithms employed in project development, testing approaches, use cases, stakeholder involvement, and the subsequent results and discussions. The project's overarching narrative is presented through a collective Group Final Report and four Individual Final Reports, providing a holistic perspective on the entire project.
